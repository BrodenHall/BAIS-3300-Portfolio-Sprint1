Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Cookies help websites function properly, remember preferences, and measure usage patterns.

2. How This Site Uses Cookies

This Site uses cookies primarily for analytics. Google Analytics may set cookies to help measure traffic and understand how visitors use the Site (for example, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referral source). Analytics information is used to improve the Site and is generally reviewed in aggregate.

3. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings, including blocking or deleting cookies. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Delete cookies already stored on your device
  • Block all cookies or block cookies from specific sites
  • Configure settings for cookie behavior

If you disable cookies, some features may not function as intended and analytics reporting may be limited.

4. More Information

For additional details about how information is handled on this Site, see the Privacy Policy.
