Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Site uses cookies and similar technologies to help understand how visitors use the Site and to improve performance. The primary tool used is Google Analytics.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences and measure usage patterns.

Cookies We Use

Google Analytics may set cookies to collect usage information such as pages visited, session duration, and referral source. This data is generally aggregated and used for analytics reporting.

How to Control Cookies

  • You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings.
  • Most browsers allow you to set rules for cookies on a per-site basis.
  • Using private browsing or tracking protection may reduce cookie-based tracking.
